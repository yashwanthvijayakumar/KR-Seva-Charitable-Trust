About Us

“Love and kindness are never wasted. They always make a difference. They bless the one who receives them, and they bless you, the giver." Barbara de Angelis

KR Seva Charitable Trust is a registered (2015-16) non-governmental, non-religious and non-profitable social service organization working with the sole objective to Provide Food, Shelter, Cloth rehabilitation and Employment Opportunities to the Poor, Sick and Disabled.

We have been successfully rendering service to humane for the past many years in and around Bangalore and Karnataka.
